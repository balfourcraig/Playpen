Gradient style

"Seed of Life" pattern drawn on an HTML5 canvas, without re-drawing any lines

It is based on a very famous part of sacred geometry. The overlapping circles can continue to infinity in all directions

It can also be created with only a compass. Simple draw a circle, then place the compass on that circle without changing its size and draw another circle. Place the point where those two intersect and draw another. You can repeat this on every intersection, with each new circle forming more intersections which expand the pattern.
